Biography

Welcome to my homepage. I’m currently an associate professor at Shanghai University. I received my B.E., Ph.D. and postdoc degrees from the EE department at Tsinghua University, China in 2015 and 2010, respectively. My advisor was Prof. Zhisheng Niu from Niulab, Tsinghua University. I visited the WiDeS group at University of Southern California from September 2013 to April 2014, advised by Prof. A. F. Molisch and Prof. Giuseppe Caire. I then worked as an experienced researcher at Ericsson from 2015 to 2016. I visited ARNG at University of Southern California, Los Angeles, USA, working with Prof. Bhaskar Krishnamachari from 2017 to 2018. I worked as a wireless signal processing scientist at Intel Labs, Hillsboro, USA during 2018.
